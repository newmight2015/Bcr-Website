What is Bitcredit

Bitcredit is the first officially accepted cypto-currency by BitBank, it features fast transaction times and stable dimensions which make it ideal as a starting point. Making use of an ASIC Hard Algorithm , Bitcredit becomes the perfect currency whose miners can either be hobbyists, Employed miners or even companies. Owning Bitcredit allows you to enjoy the benefits of an anonymous decentralized currency and utilize the facilities built around it. As Bitcredit grows and diversifies extra caveats will pop up - you can invest in any project using your Bitcredits.

Income on the Block Chain

Bank Nodes are specially configured clients that allow their owners to provide services to other users. Bank Nodes earn their owners profits through collection of fees, stipends awarded by the blockchain, and for the best performing nodes, you could earn a salary paid by the block chain. Bitcredit has an extensive initiative that ensures that the block chain will eventuall become self maintaining. We have a in built voting system that allows users to select candidates to fill certain jobs such as leadership, marketing or support. This means that users have many options to choose form in order to earn coins, you can be a miner, run a Bank or be employed by the blockchain, to help maintain it and increase awareness. Startinbg from the launch of bank nodes, major decisions will be made through democratic vote, this enables every user to have a say in the direction that the project takes. Involvement and participation are key to growing a community and we understand and agree with that.
